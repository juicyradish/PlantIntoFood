Us and Our Project


We are a group of passionate undergraduate students who want to do our best to help people in our society. We draws our inspiration from an app called "Ant Forest", in which people can earn points though various methods, whenever they take a step to reduce their emissions, and the points can be used to plant trees.



Our project is aiming for helping homeless people in our society, as well as promoting a healthier lifestyle. There are various ways you can earn "supports points". For the details, please go to the "Points-System" section. You can use the points to redeem items, such as food, daily supplies, etc, and we will donate those food to people in need! After you've donated your food, you will receive corresponding "achievement points", and you can use those points to change for small gifts!
Also, you will be able to see the plant photos you've uploaded, and you can make your unique photo collections for your cute baby plants!


You can earn "support-points" from

  1. growing your plants and uploading the photos to our websites
  2. Volunteering, and submit your proof for the work you've done (a written proof from your supervisor)
  3. Write about your fun experience through planting and volunteering

  • You can submit up to 3 photos per day, and you will receive 2 points for submitting the photos each time
  • You can get 3 points for each hour of volunteering
  • You can write up to one post per day, and for each post you wrote and shared, you will get 2 points
