Us and Our Project


We are a group of passionate undergraduate students who want to do our best to help people in our society. We draw our inspiration from an app called "Ant Forest", which people can earn points through various methods, whenever they take a step to reduce their emissions, and they can use their points to plant trees.



Our project has two main goals: to help homeless people in our society and to promote a healthier lifestyle. There are various ways you can earn "supports points"; please go to the "Points-System" section to see the details. You can use the points to redeem items, such as food, daily supplies, and we"ll donate that food to people in need! After you've donated your food, you will receive corresponding "achievement points". You can use those points to change for small gifts!
Also, you will be able to see the plant photos you've uploaded, and you can make your unique photo collections for your cute baby plants!


You can earn "support-points" from

  1. growing your plants and uploading the photos to our websites
  2. Volunteering, and submit your proof for the work you've done (a written proof from your supervisor)
  3. Write about your fun experience through planting and volunteering

  • You can submit up to 3 photos per day, and you will receive 2 points for submitting the photos each time
  • You can get 3 points for each hour of volunteering
  • You can write up to one post per day, and for each post you wrote and shared, you will get 2 points
